# Muenchen — Image Descriptions

## 1. Hero Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/muenchen/hero.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080
- **Type:** Hero image
- **Description:** Munich skyline with the Alps visible in the background. The image should feature recognizable Munich elements (Frauenkirche towers, modern high-rises, or BMW Welt area) while conveying a tech-forward identity. A subtle digital overlay — network graphics, data flow lines, or geometric patterns — can be blended into the sky or across the cityscape.
- **Style:** Professional, modern, tech-focused. Warm southern German light (golden or late afternoon) contrasting with cool digital overlay elements. High resolution, cinematic. Should feel premium and innovative — matching Munich's reputation as a high-tech, high-quality city.
- **Notes:** The Alps backdrop is a distinctive differentiator from other German city pages. Ensure the mountains are visible but the city remains the focal point. Avoid overly traditional/Bavarian imagery (no Lederhosen, no Oktoberfest).
