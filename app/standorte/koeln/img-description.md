# Koeln — Image Descriptions

## 1. Hero Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/koeln/hero.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Hero image
- **Description:** Cologne skyline featuring the Koelner Dom (Cathedral) with the modern Rhine riverfront. The shot should include the Rheinauhafen area or MediaPark district to balance the historic cathedral with contemporary architecture (Kranhaus buildings are ideal). The Rhine river should be visible, connecting old and new Cologne.
- **Style:** Professional, modern, dynamic. Evening or blue hour lighting works well to show the city illuminated. Rich colors, architectural contrast between Gothic cathedral and modern glass/steel buildings. Should feel urban, vibrant, and forward-looking.
- **Notes:** The Dom should be recognizable but the image must not feel like a tourist postcard. Include modern elements to signal that Cologne is a business and tech city.

## 2. Medien (Media) Section Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/koeln/medien.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Section image
- **Description:** TV production studio or broadcasting facility — professional cameras, studio lighting rigs, control rooms with multiple monitors, or a live production set. Should represent Cologne's position as Germany's TV capital (RTL, WDR, numerous production companies in the MediaPark and MMC studios).
- **Style:** Professional, high-tech media production. Dramatic studio lighting, screens glowing, equipment in use. Should feel active and professional, not empty or staged.
- **Notes:** A behind-the-scenes production view is more interesting than a generic office. Control rooms with banks of monitors work particularly well.

## 3. Versicherung (Insurance) Section Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/koeln/versicherung.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Section image
- **Description:** Modern office buildings in Cologne's insurance district — clean corporate architecture, glass facades, possibly the headquarters area of major insurers (AXA, DEVK, Gothaer). Could also show a modern open-plan office interior with professionals at work, representing the insurance and financial services sector.
- **Style:** Professional, corporate-modern. Clean lines, neutral colors, well-lit. Should feel trustworthy, established, and contemporary — not stuffy or old-fashioned.
- **Notes:** Insurance is a major industry in Cologne. The image should communicate digital transformation in a traditionally conservative sector.

## 4. Chemie (Chemistry) Section Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/koeln/chemie.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Section image
- **Description:** Chemical plant or industrial facility — pipes, reactors, towers, possibly the Chempark Leverkusen/Dormagen area visible from or near Cologne. Could also show a modern chemical laboratory with researchers and analytical equipment. The image should represent the chemical and pharmaceutical industry cluster in the Cologne/Rhine region.
- **Style:** Professional, industrial-modern. Clean and organized (not gritty or polluted-looking). Steel and white tones, possibly with blue or green accent lighting. Should feel like advanced science and engineering.
- **Notes:** Bayer, Lanxess, and INEOS are major employers in the region. The image should convey high-tech industry, safety, and precision — not environmental concern.
