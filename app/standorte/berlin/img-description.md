# Berlin — Image Descriptions

## 1. Hero Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/berlin/hero.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080
- **Type:** Hero image
- **Description:** Berlin skyline featuring the Fernsehturm (TV Tower) prominently. The image should convey a modern, tech-forward city. Consider a subtle digital overlay — network node graphics, data streams, or geometric patterns — blended into the sky or cityscape to emphasize Berlin as a technology hub.
- **Style:** Professional, modern, tech-focused. Cool blue and warm golden tones (evening light works well). High resolution, cinematic aspect ratio. Should feel aspirational and forward-looking.
- **Notes:** Avoid overly touristy angles. Prefer a vantage point that shows both the iconic tower and modern architecture (e.g., from Potsdamer Platz area or looking across the Spree).

## 2. Innovation Section Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/berlin/innovation.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Section image
- **Description:** A modern industrial/tech facility representing Berlin's innovation landscape — ideally Tesla Gigafactory Gruenheide or Siemensstadt 2.0. Alternatively, a contemporary tech campus with glass facades, solar panels, and a clean industrial aesthetic. The image should communicate large-scale innovation and manufacturing meeting digital transformation.
- **Style:** Professional, modern, clean lines. Bright daylight, sharp focus. Should feel like cutting-edge industry, not old-school factory. Neutral to cool color palette with industrial accents.
- **Notes:** If a specific facility image is not available, a generic modern tech campus or innovation hub in the Berlin-Brandenburg region works as a substitute.
