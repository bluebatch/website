# Hamburg — Image Descriptions

## 1. Hero Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/hamburg/hero.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Hero image
- **Description:** Hamburg harbor panorama with container ships and cranes in the foreground and the Elbphilharmonie concert hall visible in the background. The image should capture the blend of traditional maritime industry and modern architecture that defines Hamburg.
- **Style:** Professional, modern, slightly dramatic lighting (golden hour or blue hour). Rich, saturated colors. Should feel dynamic and prosperous.
- **Notes:** Wide-angle shot preferred. The Elbphilharmonie's glass wave structure should be recognizable but doesn't need to dominate the frame.

## 2. Hafen (Harbor) Tab Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/hamburg/hafen.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Tab image
- **Description:** Container terminal in the Port of Hamburg — large container cranes, stacked shipping containers, and cargo ships. A subtle digital logistics overlay (data points, route lines, or IoT indicators) can be composited to emphasize smart logistics and automation.
- **Style:** Professional, industrial-modern. Strong geometric lines from cranes and containers. Cool blue and steel tones with accent colors from container branding.
- **Notes:** Should feel large-scale and operational, not static. Movement (ships, cranes) adds energy.

## 3. Airbus Tab Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/hamburg/airbus.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Tab image
- **Description:** Airbus facility in Hamburg-Finkenwerder or aircraft production/assembly line. Could show an aircraft fuselage in a hangar, assembly robots, or the exterior of the Airbus plant with aircraft on the apron.
- **Style:** Professional, high-tech industrial. Clean, well-lit (hangar lighting or bright exterior). Should communicate precision engineering and advanced manufacturing.
- **Notes:** If direct Airbus imagery is unavailable, a generic aerospace manufacturing or aircraft assembly scene works.

## 4. Medien (Media) Tab Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/hamburg/medien.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Tab image
- **Description:** Media production environment — a modern newsroom, TV studio control room, or publishing workspace. Screens displaying content, professional cameras, editing equipment. Should represent Hamburg's role as Germany's media capital (Gruner+Jahr, Spiegel, NDR).
- **Style:** Professional, contemporary. Warm artificial lighting mixed with screen glow. Dynamic, busy atmosphere. Modern equipment and clean workspace design.
- **Notes:** Avoid dated-looking newsroom setups. Should feel current and digital-first.

## 5. E-Commerce Tab Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/hamburg/ecommerce.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Tab image
- **Description:** E-commerce fulfillment center or warehouse — conveyor belts, shelving systems, packages being sorted or prepared for shipping. Could also show a modern logistics hub with automated picking systems (Otto Group, About You style operations).
- **Style:** Professional, modern logistics. Well-organized, efficient atmosphere. Bright warehouse lighting. Should communicate scale and automation.
- **Notes:** Branded packages or recognizable e-commerce elements help contextualize the image.

## 6. Smart Port Section Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/hamburg/smartport.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Section image
- **Description:** Digital/smart port operations — automated guided vehicles (AGVs), IoT sensors on containers or cranes, a port control center with digital dashboards, or an aerial view of an automated terminal (e.g., CTA Altenwerder). The image should convey the digital transformation of port logistics.
- **Style:** Professional, futuristic-industrial. Cool color palette with digital accent elements (blue data overlays, glowing sensor points). Should feel like the intersection of heavy industry and cutting-edge technology.
- **Notes:** Hamburg's smartPORT initiative is a reference point. Automated container handling and real-time data visualization are key visual themes.
