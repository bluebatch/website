# Frankfurt am Main — Image Descriptions

## 1. Hero Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/frankfurt/hero.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080
- **Type:** Hero image
- **Description:** Frankfurt skyline ("Mainhattan") at dusk or night — skyscrapers lit up, reflecting in the Main river. Key buildings: Commerzbank Tower, Messeturm, European Central Bank, Deutsche Bank towers. The image should capture Frankfurt's unique position as the only German city with a true high-rise skyline.
- **Style:** Professional, dramatic, premium. Deep blue/purple sky transitioning to warm golden building lights. High contrast, cinematic. Should feel like a global financial center — powerful, sophisticated, and modern.
- **Notes:** The dusk/night setting is important for the dramatic lighting effect on the skyscrapers. A view from the Eiserner Steg bridge or the south bank of the Main provides an iconic composition.

## 2. Finance Tab Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/frankfurt/finance.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Tab image
- **Description:** Banking district or trading floor environment — financial data screens, Bloomberg terminals, professionals in a modern trading room, or the exterior of the Frankfurt Stock Exchange (Boerse Frankfurt). Could also show the ECB building with its distinctive twisted towers.
- **Style:** Professional, high-energy, data-rich. Blue and green screen glows, sharp focus on data displays. Should feel fast-paced, analytical, and globally connected.
- **Notes:** Frankfurt is Europe's financial hub. The image should communicate both traditional banking strength and modern fintech innovation.

## 3. Airport Tab Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/frankfurt/airport.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Tab image
- **Description:** Frankfurt Airport (Fraport) — terminal interior with modern architecture, departure boards, aircraft visible through windows, or the apron with planes and ground logistics. Could also show air cargo operations, as Frankfurt is Europe's largest cargo airport.
- **Style:** Professional, expansive, operational. Bright terminal lighting, large glass facades, sense of movement and scale. Clean architectural lines.
- **Notes:** Frankfurt Airport is a massive employer and logistics hub. Both passenger and cargo aspects are relevant. The image should convey international connectivity and logistical complexity.

## 4. Messe Tab Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/frankfurt/messe.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Tab image
- **Description:** Messe Frankfurt exhibition halls — trade fair in action with exhibition stands, visitors, or the iconic Messeturm and Festhalle exterior. Could show a technology trade fair (like Automechanika or Light+Building) with modern booth designs, interactive displays, and professional crowds.
- **Style:** Professional, event-scale, dynamic. Bright exhibition lighting, colorful booth graphics, busy atmosphere. Should feel like a global meeting point for industry.

## 5. Fintech Tab Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/frankfurt/fintech.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Tab image
- **Description:** Modern coworking or startup space — open floor plan with exposed brick/concrete, standing desks, whiteboards with diagrams, developers at screens. Should evoke the TechQuartier or Frankfurt fintech hub atmosphere. Young professionals, collaborative environment, laptops and big screens.
- **Style:** Professional but relaxed startup aesthetic. Warm wood and industrial materials, natural light, plants. Should feel innovative, agile, and creative — contrasting with the traditional banking imagery.
- **Notes:** Frankfurt's fintech scene is growing rapidly. The image should show the newer, startup side of Frankfurt's financial ecosystem, distinct from the corporate banking world.

## 6. Rhein-Main Region Section Image

- **Filename:** `/standorte/frankfurt/rheinmain.jpg`
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800
- **Type:** Section image
- **Description:** Aerial or elevated panoramic view of the Rhein-Main metropolitan region — showing the landscape where the Rhine and Main rivers converge, with cities, infrastructure (highways, rail), and green spaces visible. Alternatively, a stylized map visualization of the region highlighting key cities (Frankfurt, Wiesbaden, Mainz, Darmstadt, Offenbach) and transport connections.
- **Style:** Professional, geographic, expansive. Bright daylight for aerial shots, or clean design for map-style visualizations. Should communicate regional connectivity and economic scale.
- **Notes:** The Rhein-Main region is one of Europe's most important economic areas. The image should convey that Frankfurt is not just a city but the center of a large, interconnected metropolitan region with 5.8 million people.
